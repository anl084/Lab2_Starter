Apples are tasty, and keeps the doctors away, only if you are careful

How to cut an apple

Preparation

First we wash the apple with water

WARNING ! Do not wash it with dishsoap

Then we wash the knife

This you can wash with dishsoap

Then we can wash the cutting board, make sure to wash all the dishsoap off


The post preparation work

  1. Then we put the apple on the cutting board
  2. The you position the knife and your hands
    • When cutting make sure your fingers isn't directly under where the knife is
    • Also make sure you dont cut your hand as you chop the apple
    • Continue until you are satisified
  3. Or you can just skip that step and just eat it without cutting it
